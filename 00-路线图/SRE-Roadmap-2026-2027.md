# 1. Why I Choose SRE

## Current Background

I have 8 years of IT operations experience.

My current role focuses on:

- Desktop support
- Windows system maintenance
- Hardware/software troubleshooting
- Basic network support


During my career, I have accumulated experience in:

- Enterprise IT environment
- User support
- System troubleshooting
- Infrastructure maintenance


## Why SRE

With the rapid development of cloud computing, automation, and AI technologies, traditional IT operations roles are facing increasing challenges.

I want to transition from traditional IT support to SRE/DevOps because:

- SRE combines operations experience with software engineering practices.
- Automation can improve operation efficiency.
- Cloud native technologies are becoming important infrastructure skills.
- SRE engineers solve reliability and scalability problems.


My goal is not only to maintain systems, but to build reliable, automated, and scalable infrastructure.
