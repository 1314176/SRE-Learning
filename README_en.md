## Why SRE?

Traditional IT operations mainly focus on system maintenance and incident handling.

Modern infrastructure also requires:

- Automation instead of repetitive manual work
- Observability instead of blind troubleshooting
- Engineering solutions instead of temporary fixes
- Reliable and scalable system architecture
- Continuous improvement based on measurable results

My goal is to move from:

> Keeping systems running

to:

> Building reliable systems
